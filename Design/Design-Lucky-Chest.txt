Zombies Design - Lucky Chest

The Lucky Chest stays at Apartments, and it costs 1000 gold to open it.

And it starts to scroll the Weapons (special ones!), and you have infinite time to choose a Weapon.

Controls to Accept the Weapon in the Lucky Chest:

PC - Left Click the Lucky Chest or press G. (see Gameplay)
Mobile - Touch the Lucky Chest.
