Zombies Design - Enchanting

The Enchantment Machine stays at Garden, and costs 1000 gold to enchant a weapon.

Thare are 5 levels of Enchanting:
Expressed in Roman Numerals (I II III IV V)

Level 1: Damage: +50%,  Use Cooldown -50%
Level 2: Damage: +100%, Use Cooldown -60%
Level 3: Damage: +150%, Use Cooldown -70%
Level 4: Damage: +200%, Use Cooldown -80%
Level 5: Damage: +300%, Use Cooldown -90%
